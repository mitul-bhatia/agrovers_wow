### Question: Farmer brother, smell the soil - what does it smell like?

**Why smell matters:**
- Indicates soil health
- Good smell = healthy soil
- Bad smell = problem
- Shows organic matter presence

---

### How to smell:

#### Step 1: Take Fresh Soil
- Dig 4-6 inches deep
- Take handful of soil

#### Step 2: Smell
- Bring soil near nose
- Take deep breath
- Smell for 5-10 seconds

**What it means:**
